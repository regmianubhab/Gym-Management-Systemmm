Our Story

FitFlow started as a college project to solve the manual entry problems faced by local gyms in Itahari. Today, it stands as a conceptual model for a fully automated fitness environment.


Problem Statement: Most gyms still use registers. This leads to data loss, payment confusion, and poor member retention.
